 What are the basic concepts of computer science?
     
The topics covered include the theory of computation
(what computers can compute),algorithms for efficient
computation(what to tell a computer to compute),
languages (how to tell a computer what to compute),
artificial intelligence(how your computercan do things
your brain does), computational biology.
        
        
What is programming in simple words?
     
ogramming refers to a technological process for
telling a computer which tasks to perform in 
order to solve problems. You can think of
