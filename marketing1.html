SEO

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization which is the process of optimizing a website to improve its visibility and ranking on search engine results pages (SERPs). This involves various strategies such as keyword research, creating quality content, building backlinks, improving user experience, optimizing titles and meta descriptions, and technical optimization of the website structure and code. The goal of SEO is to increase organic (non-paid) traffic to the website, ultimately leading to higher online visibility and reaching a wider audience.
