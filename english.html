Why English?

There are Many reasons why english is so important in our lives such as:

  • It is a global language, allowing you to communicate with people from different cultures
  • It opens up job opportunities and helps you study in the best schools worldwide
  • English is the language of the media industry and the Internet
  • It facilitates travel and understanding of other languages
  • Learning English exercises your brain and helps you make new friends

Also as of my last update in January 2022, it's estimated that over 1.5 billion people speak English worldwide.
This includes native speakers, second language speakers, and speakers of English as a foreign language.
